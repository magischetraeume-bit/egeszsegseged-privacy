# Privacy Policy – Egészség Segéd

Last updated: 2026-01-11

## 1. Introduction
Egészség Segéd ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

## 2. Data Collection
The app may collect and store the following data locally on the user’s device:
- Medication names, schedules, and intake confirmations
- Inventory and reminder-related information
- Health-related measurements entered manually by the user

The app does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

## 3. Advertisements
The free version of the app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.
AdMob may collect anonymous usage data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Users can optionally purchase an ad-free version.

## 4. Data Sharing
Egészség Segéd does not share personal or health-related data with third parties.

## 5. Data Security
All data is stored locally on the device. Users are responsible for device security, backups, and access protection.

## 6. Children’s Privacy
The app is not specifically targeted at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 7. Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Changes will be published on this page.

## 8. Contact
If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing.
